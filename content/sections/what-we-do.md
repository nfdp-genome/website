### Unlocking Potential: Healthier and productive Animals, Sustainable Practices

We aim to transform the industry in the kingdom and enable data driven economy by accelerating genetic improvement and protecting livestock and fisheries from threats of pathogens. Transforming the domain requires a strategic alignment and enablement from the national strategy of biotechnology, as NFLDP embracing biotech as the main thrust for its progress.
